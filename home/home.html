Kankali secondary school

We believe that education is a factor which brings positive change on human behavior in the society. Students from various socio-economic and cultural backgrounds are being benefited as well as share common bond from this school. KSS is offering the quality education to the lower-middle class families, marginalized and deprived community. We assist students to have self-discipline along with self-respect. Also, we encourage learning for excellence. The school is able to meet the emerging technological development to fulfill the demand of globalization, to improve students' learning, enhance quality education, physical and managerial aspects, to identify & maximize the use of human resources for the entire development of KSS.

Since 2073 (2015) we have started technical and vocational stream in computer engineering. We have been providing residential facilities for the technical students. Our vision is to provide the quality education through model school. For this, we have been seeking such helping hands for the further development of KSS.
