ABOUT US

Located in Kathmandu valley at the altitude of 1600m from sea level in an attractive clean, green, peace and safety environment Kankali Secondary School (KSS) has been providing adequate education with the support of national and international personalities. KSS was founded on 25th Baisakh 2039 B.S (08th May 1982) and passed a long journey of many ups and downs for the last 36 years. There are altogether 292 students among which 128 are male and 164 are female. We are offering scholarship to 61 students who are excellent in their study and who have weak financial background. We are offering academic education from Nursery to Grade 9 and TVET program from Grade 9. Our school have altogether 36 staffs. Our school has wide range of excellent facilities including library, computer lab, transportation, CCTV in every classroom. Our students are highly active in extra curricular activities that has enhance them in learning and exploring different new interest and motivating them to go school regularly.

Mission: - Enriching the quality of education and encouraging students to learn in a friendly and creative environment for the intellectual development of a student.
